I love a good story. I have heard and seen many stories in my lifetime. Here are some pictures that depict the kinds of stories that I love.

I grew up with a dad who loves science-fiction stories, such as Star Trek and Star Wars. Because of that, I love to read science-fiction and fantasy stories. But, there are still other kinds of stories out there.

My mom always encouraged me to read classic stories. My favorite kind of classic story is the romantic kind, Pride and Prejudice being one of the best. Since reading that, I have read many other classic tales. I have also read and seen a bit of Shakespeare's poetry and plays, thanks to my parents' love of theatre. But romance and sci-fi aren't the only genres that intrigue me.

I love a good mystery. Naturally, I have read The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, as well as some new Sherlock stories by modern authors. I've read so many of his adventures that sometimes I find myself coming up with my own solution to the problem before Sherlock does.
